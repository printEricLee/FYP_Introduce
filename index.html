Smart Compliance for Construction Waste Disposal

This is a collaboration project between HKIIT and Chun Wo Construction & Engineering Company Limited. It aims to revolutionize the compliance workflow of dump truck disposal operations for Hong Kong's construction industry. The project initiative is to automate the monitoring of dump wastes on trucks by Al vision to detect violations such as overloading, proper coverage of material and contamination of foreign objects. The process is integrated into the original inspection workflow to streamline the operations, minimize the labor costs and ensure robust regulatory adherence for dump truck disposal.
